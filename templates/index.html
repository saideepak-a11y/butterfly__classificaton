Enchanted Wings: Butterfly Classification

About This

The Enchanted Wings: Butterfly Classification web application is a smart visual recognition tool built to classify different species of butterflies. It uses deep learning, specifically the powerful VGG16 neural network, to analyze and identify butterflies from uploaded images.

The model was trained on a rich dataset of over 70 butterfly species. Its training involved thousands of images, enabling the system to understand unique wing patterns, shapes, and colors to predict species with high accuracy.
